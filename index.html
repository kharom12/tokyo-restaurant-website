Our Story

Meet Snow私たちの物語

Years ago, Snow was just a hard-working college student, juggling late-night shifts at a small boba tea shop to pay for tuition, books, and the life she was building one cup at a time.

Between sealing cups and shaking milk tea, she held a quiet dream in her heart — “One day, I'll have a boba shop of my own. A place that feels like home.”

She didn't imagine that dream would grow even bigger. Today, that little wish has blossomed into Tokyo Express — Hibachi and Boba, a place where the sizzle of a hibachi grill meets the happy pop of tapioca pearls. Every plate we serve and every cup we shake is poured with the same spirit that started it all: hustle, heart, and a hope that a good meal can make someone's day a little brighter.

Whether you're stopping in for a quick lunch between classes, bringing the family for dinner, or catering your next big event — welcome. You're part of the story now.
