Our Story

Meet Snow私たちの物語

Long before Tokyo Express, a young college girl named Snow was pulling late shifts at a hibachi Japanese restaurant — running plates of sizzling steak and shrimp, learning the rhythm of the grill, and saving every dollar to pay for tuition, books, and a future she was still dreaming up.

Between orders, she kept a quiet wish tucked close to her heart — “One day, I'll have a little hibachi place of my own. A place that feels like home.”

That was many years ago. College came and went. Life brought her a husband, two beautiful children, a home in Greensboro, and more kitchens than she ever imagined cooking in. The dream grew up right alongside her — and bloomed into Tokyo Express — Hibachi and Boba, where the sizzle of the grill meets the happy pop of tapioca pearls.

Today, every plate Snow serves and every cup she shakes carries the same spirit that started it all: hustle, heart, and the love of a mom who believes a good meal can make someone's day a little brighter.

Whether you're stopping in for a quick lunch, bringing the whole family for dinner, or catering your next big celebration — welcome. You're part of the story now.
